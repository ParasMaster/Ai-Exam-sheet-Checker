AI-Powered Answer-Sheet Grading System

A fresh project idea under development: An AI-driven platform concept that processes handwritten and typed answers, enables semantic scoring, and supports real-time feedback with educator oversight. This is a newly conceived idea with no existing models or prototypes yet.

Initial Concept: Basic Foundations

The core idea begins with simple OCR for printed text and keyword-based matching to automate initial grading tasks. This foundational approach aims to handle straightforward assessments while identifying areas for manual review.

Quick Facts

  • OCR Engine: Basic Tesseract for printed text
  • Matching Method: Rule-based keywords
  • Focus: Automating simple tasks
  • Limitations: Conceptual; no handwriting support yet

Enhanced Concept: Adding Semantics

Building on the basics, the idea incorporates advanced OCR for handwritten text and semantic embeddings to understand context and synonyms, allowing for more flexible scoring.

Quick Facts

  • OCR Engine: Planned Google Vision with HTR
  • Matching Method: BERT-based embeddings
  • Goal: Flexible partial credit
  • Status: Under development

Proposed System: Hybrid AI & Human — Core Components

The envisioned pipeline includes OCR/HTR for text extraction and transformer-based matching for semantic evaluation.

Confidence scoring would flag uncertain cases for human review, combining AI efficiency with expert oversight.

Highlights

  • OCR: Multi-engine integration planned
  • Semantic Matching: Transformer models

Proposed System: Hybrid AI & Human — Efficiency Features

Adaptive thresholds for automated decisions, with human-in-the-loop for quality assurance.

The concept aims to streamline grading while maintaining accuracy through balanced automation.

Highlights

  • Efficiency: Automated where possible
  • Review: Human oversight integrated
